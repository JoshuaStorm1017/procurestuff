# ProcureStuff Specification

## Paid Product Being Replaced

ProcureStuff is a free, self-hosted alternative to Zip, Coupa, Procurify, and intake-to-procure modules bundled into enterprise spend platforms.

## User Pain And Pricing Lock-In

Procurement suites often price by requester, approver, workflow module, supplier module, and spend volume. Small teams still need intake, approvals, supplier context, purchase orders, budget visibility, and audit evidence, but their records can become trapped behind expensive hosted workflows and limited exports.

## Anti-Lock-In Headline

Own procurement intake, suppliers, approvals, purchase orders, budgets, and audit history in portable local JSON and CSV.

## Exact User Workflows

1. Review the dashboard for active requests, requested spend, pending approvals, renewals due soon, and recent activity.
2. Create, edit, search, filter, sort, inspect, archive, and restore purchase requests.
3. Advance purchase requests through submitted, approved, ordered, received, closed, or rejected states.
4. Maintain supplier records with category, owner, lifecycle status, risk, renewal date, website, and notes.
5. Manage approval tasks with request linkage, approver, status, due date, and decision notes.
6. Track purchase orders with request, supplier, PO number, status, total, expected date, and delivery notes.
7. Track budgets with owner, fiscal period, approved amount, committed amount, status, and notes.
8. Export every entity as JSON or CSV, export the full workspace snapshot, and import a workspace snapshot.

## Data Model

- Workspace: organization, procurement policy, approval cadence, spend categories, finance reviewers.
- Purchase requests: requester, department, category, status, supplier, budget, amount, needed date, justification, tags.
- Suppliers: category, owner, status, risk, renewal date, website, notes.
- Approvals: request, approver, status, due date, decision notes.
- Purchase orders: request, supplier, PO number, status, total, expected date, delivery notes.
- Budgets: owner, fiscal period, approved amount, committed amount, status, notes.
- Activity: append-only local audit records generated by workflow and data changes.

## Must-Have Screens

- Dashboard with computed metrics, request breakdown, priority queue, export links, and activity.
- Requests workbench with table controls, detail inspector, workflow action, archive, and restore.
- Suppliers, Approvals, Orders, and Budgets workbenches with complete CRUD and filtering.
- Settings screen for policy, cadence, categories, finance reviewers, import, and export.
- Modal editors for all collections and workspace import.

## Local / Self-Hosted Behavior

- Runs with Node 24 and no paid procurement platform credentials.
- Persists state to `data/procurestuff.json` by default.
- Supports `PROCURESTUFF_DATA=/path/to/file.json` for custom storage.
- Uses atomic JSON writes for local durability.
- Keeps request, supplier, approval, order, budget, and activity records portable through full workspace JSON and per-collection JSON/CSV export.

## Acceptance Criteria

- All collections support create, read, update, archive, restore, search, filter, sort, and detail inspection.
- Required fields, date, URL, numeric, select, and relation validation run server-side.
- Dashboard metrics are computed from persisted records.
- Workflow actions mutate request, approval, and purchase order state while recording activity.
- Workspace import/export works without external credentials.
- README, MIT license, scripts, seed data, tests, build, lint, and secret scan are present.
- No existing non-Codex projects are edited.
